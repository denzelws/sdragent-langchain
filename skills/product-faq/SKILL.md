# Product FAQ Skill

Use this skill when a prospect or customer asks about product details, pricing, plans, integrations, free trials, refunds, contracts, or policies.

## Pricing tiers

### Starter

$299/month.

Includes:

- up to 3 users
- 10 workflows
- HubSpot + Stripe integrations only

### Growth

$799/month.

Includes:

- up to 10 users
- unlimited workflows
- all standard integrations

### Enterprise

Enterprise uses custom pricing and is intended for larger teams or companies that need advanced security, support, reliability, or custom integration requirements.

Includes:

- SSO
- dedicated support
- SLA
- custom integrations

Important Enterprise policies:

- Enterprise contracts are annual only.
- Enterprise does not have a standard monthly price.
- If a prospect asks whether Enterprise is monthly or annual, answer that Enterprise uses annual contracts only.
- If a prospect asks about Enterprise pricing, answer that Enterprise uses custom pricing and offer a 15-minute call to discuss requirements.
- If a prospect asks about SSO, dedicated support, SLA, or custom integrations, confirm that these are available on Enterprise.

## Policies

- Starter and Growth include a 14-day free trial.
- Enterprise uses annual contracts only.
- Enterprise has custom pricing.
- No refunds after 30 days.
- If the sender asks about refunds, clearly state that there are no refunds after 30 days.
- If the sender asks about a policy not listed here, do not invent an answer. Offer to confirm it on a 15-minute call.

## Safety behavior

- If the user asks about something not covered by this skill, say that you can confirm it on a quick 15-minute call instead of inventing an answer.
- Do not promise discounts, custom terms, implementation timelines, or refund exceptions.
- If the question is about an unsupported or unclear integration, explain only what is known from the pricing tiers.

## Reply behavior

When answering a pricing or product question:

- answer confidently
- be concise
- do not invent pricing
- do not invent policies
- answer every product, pricing, or policy question the sender explicitly asks
- mention only the relevant plan details
- if the sender asks about pricing or plan comparison, always include the relevant plan prices
- if the sender asks about Enterprise, mention the relevant Enterprise facts: custom pricing, annual contracts only, SSO, dedicated support, SLA, or custom integrations
- if the sender asks about refunds, mention that there are no refunds after 30 days
- do not mention Enterprise unless the sender asks about Enterprise, custom pricing, SSO, SLA, dedicated support, annual contracts, or custom integrations
- always offer to book a 15-minute call if they want to learn more
- sign with the configured sender name
